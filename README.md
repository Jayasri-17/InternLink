# InternLink

## **Project Overview**

**InternLink** is a web-based platform designed to help students discover, apply for, and manage internship opportunities. Users can upload internship links, browse available internships, apply directly, and save them for later reference.

---

## **Key Features**

- **Upload and access internship opportunities**
- **Apply for internships directly**
- **Save internships for later**
- **Track applied and saved internships**
- **AI-powered chatbot for queries**
- **Interview preparation guidance**
- **Resume building tips**
- **Share internship links on social media**

---

## **Technologies Used**

- **HTML**
- **CSS**
- **JavaScript**
- **Firebase**
- **AI Chatbot Integration**

---

## **Objective**

The objective of **InternLink** is to simplify the internship search process by providing a centralized and intelligent platform with chatbot assistance.
